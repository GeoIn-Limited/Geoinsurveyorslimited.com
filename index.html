Topographical Survey and Beacon Establishment

We use topographical survey to create a detailed map of the natural and man-made features of a piece of land. This includes things like elevations, contours, bodies of water, vegetation, and man-made structures. These features are depicted on the survey using symbols, contour lines, and spot elevations. We use beacon survey to determine the position of an object or location. The beacons are typically placed at known locations and used to triangulate the position of the object or location being surveyed. We often us this survey in navigation and mapping applications.
